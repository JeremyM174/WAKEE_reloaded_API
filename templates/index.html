WAKEE.reloaded's image analysis

Hello, and welcome to WAKEE.reloaded!

WAKEE was a project about detecting cognitive drift for people living with ADHD - Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder, to help them focus on their tasks. It comes back as WAKEE.reloaded with the full automation of its data lifecycle!


What can you do with this API?

By requesting the API behind this space, you can:

  • Send an image for recognition of the emotion, preferably without accessories (such as glasses)
  • Receive a personalized suggestion to refocus on your activity, should cognitive drift be recognized (through visual markers of disengagement, frustration, confusion or boredom)
  • And finally keep it for personal use, as this is merely a proof of concept for a student project; by European standards of the AI Act, any other use would be strictly forbidden!


How to use the API?

If you want to learn more about how the API works, feel free to check its documentation. Don't worry, it's very short!


Why such a project?

WAKEE was originally made by four people: Asma RHALMI, Manon FAEDY, Albert ROMANO & Jérémy MARIAGE.

Our intent was to demonstrate machine learning is not only about making money by pillaging your personal data: it can help with issues in your daily life such as ADHD, as similar disorders in attention are being increasingly identified.

Sadly, given European regulations and the expected deviation in monetizing the concept, WAKEE cannot be and will not be more than a proof of concept tied to a student project - even if we do not store any logs and delete on purpose all ingested data!
